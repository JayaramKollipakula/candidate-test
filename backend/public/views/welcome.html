Welcome to Turco Persian

Who we are?

Turco Persian Rug Company has been operating from our original downtown Toronto location since 1906. An Armenian family-run business spanning four generations, Turco Persian continues to provide and redefine unique, honest and premium service.

What we offer?

Specializing in the cleaning and restoration of all area rugs, from antique collectables to machine-made mats, Turco Persian offers in-home carpet installation, repair and cleaning, as well as upholstery cleaning services. Our team of employees is knowledgeable, dedicated and highly trained
